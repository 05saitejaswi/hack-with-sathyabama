Hack with sathyabama ,a student-led virtual hackathon, brought to you by Sathyabam University, the Computer Science department at Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai, is bound to drive your quarantine blues away! With innovative tracks based on the need of the hour, HacK WITH Sathyabama implores all the aspiring strategists to share their vision with the community. The best part?
